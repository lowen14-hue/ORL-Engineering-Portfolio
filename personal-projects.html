🚁 Ongoing Engineering Project

FPV Drone Design & Build

Cost-efficient custom FPV drone with hardware integration, FAA compliance, and 3D printed carbon fiber frame

Project Overview

Custom FPV drone engineering project focused on designing a lightweight, cost-efficient frame that integrates with commercially available motors and flight controllers.

The design process emphasizes hardware compatibility, regulatory compliance with FAA size and weight restrictions, and iterative prototyping through 3D printing to validate component alignment before final production in carbon fiber composite material.

Based on foundational concepts from Joshua Bardwell's FPV drone DIY platform, this project has been customized with integrated motor ESCs and an alternative video capture solution using a GoPro camera instead of traditional expensive FPV equipment.

Project Phases

✓ Phase 1: Frame Design & CAD (Completed)

Initial frame geometry designed in CAD, optimized for weight distribution, motor mounting, and flight controller placement. First round of designing complete with all component locations finalized.

→ Phase 2: Hardware Integration (Current)

Ordering motors with built-in ESCs and flight controller to validate bolt holes and standoffs. Ensuring all screw holes align properly with purchased hardware before any printing occurs.

→ Phase 3: Prototype Validation

3D printing frame prototype in standard filament to verify geometry and hardware alignment. Testing fit and function of all electrical components before moving to final carbon fiber production.

→ Phase 4: Design Iteration

Likely design adjustments after hardware delivery and testing. Fine-tuning based on actual component dimensions and performance requirements discovered during prototype assembly.

→ Phase 5: Carbon Fiber Frame Production

Once geometry is validated and proven correct for hardware compatibility, printing final frame in carbon fiber filament for weight reduction and structural rigidity.

→ Phase 6: Assembly & Testing

Final assembly of all components, flight controller configuration, and ground testing before first flight operations.

Weight Target

Optimized for FAA regulations - keeping drone under critical weight thresholds for simplified regulatory compliance

Frame Design

Modular 3D printed structure with integrated motor mounting points and standardized fastener holes

Component Integration

Custom standoffs and bolt patterns designed around purchased motor and flight controller specifications

Design Philosophy

Cost-Efficient Design

Prioritizing affordable components while maintaining functional performance. Motors with integrated ESCs reduce component count and complexity.

Hardware-First Approach

Designing around purchased components ensures compatibility and eliminates costly redesigns after assembly.

Iterative Prototyping

3D printing prototypes to validate geometry before carbon fiber production, reducing waste and ensuring fit accuracy.

Alternative Video Solution

Using existing GoPro camera for video capture and live feed capability, eliminating the need for expensive dedicated FPV helmet systems.

Regulatory Compliance

Designing specifically for FAA weight and size regulations to minimize licensing complexity and operational restrictions.

Customization & Adaptation

Building upon proven FPV fundamentals while tailoring the design to personal needs and available resources.

Hardware Integration Plan

Motors:

Brushless motors with built-in ESCs for simplified wiring and reduced component integration complexity

Flight Controller:

Commercially available flight controller with standard mounting holes and connection specs

Frame Material:

Initial prototype in standard PLA/PETG, final version in carbon fiber filament for strength-to-weight optimization

Video System:

GoPro camera for high-quality video recording and live feed capability via onboard transmission

Battery System:

LiPo batteries sized for FAA compliance and optimized flight duration targets

Fasteners:

Standard metric bolts and standoffs designed into frame with precise hole placement for hardware alignment
